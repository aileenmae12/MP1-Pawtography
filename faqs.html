Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What safety measures are in place during the photoshoot?
A: We prioritize the safety and well-being of both pets and humans. Our studio is pet-friendly and equipped with safety measures such as non-toxic props, secure and comfortable spaces, and trained staff to handle pets with care.
Q: Are the photography products and equipment safe for pets?
A: Absolutely! We use pet-friendly and non-toxic materials for our photography products and props. Our equipment is pet-safe, and we take precautions to ensure that there are no hazards during the photoshoot.
Q: How do you handle anxious or nervous pets during the session?
A: We understand that pets may feel anxious or nervous in a new environment. Our experienced photographers are trained to work patiently and calmly with pets. We provide a calm and comforting atmosphere, allowing pets to adjust and feel more relaxed during the session.
Q: Do you require vaccinations for pets participating in the photoshoot?
A: For the safety of all pets involved, we require proof of up-to-date vaccinations for each pet. This helps prevent the spread of diseases and ensures a healthy environment for everyone.
